A car is a type of vehicle that is designed for road transportation. Cars are powered by an internal combustion engine or an electric motor, which provides the power to move the vehicle. The earliest cars were invented in the late 19th century and were powered by steam engines. Modern cars are made from a variety of materials, including steel, aluminium, plastic, and composite materials. Cars are typically equipped with a variety of safety features, such as seat belts, airbags, and traction control, to help protect passengers in the event of a collision. Cars are also equipped with various technologies, such as navigation systems and infotainment systems, to enhance the driving experience. There are many different types of cars, including sedans, hatchbacks, SUVs, and sports cars. Cars can be fueled by gasoline, diesel, or electricity. The cost of owning and operating a car can vary greatly depending on factors such as the make and model of the car, the cost of fuel, and the cost of maintenance and repairs. Cars are an important part of modern society and are used by individuals and businesses alike for transportation, commerce, and leisure
